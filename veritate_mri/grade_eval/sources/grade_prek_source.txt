Pat had a dog. The dog was named Rex. Rex was big. Rex was brown. Rex liked to run.

One day, Pat took Rex to the park. The park had many trees. The park had a pond. The pond had ducks. Rex saw the ducks. Rex barked. The ducks did not care. The ducks just swam.

Pat sat on a bench. Pat had a ball. Pat threw the ball. Rex ran. Rex got the ball. Rex came back. Pat said, "Good dog." Pat threw the ball again.

A girl walked by. The girl had a kite. The kite was red. The kite was big. The wind was strong. The kite went up. The kite went high. The girl ran. The girl held the string. The kite did not get away.

Rex watched the kite. Rex did not bark. Rex was tired. Rex sat down. Pat patted Rex on the head.

A boy came over. The boy had a small dog. The small dog was white. The small dog had black ears. "Can our dogs play?" the boy asked. "Yes," said Pat. The two dogs sniffed each other. The two dogs ran. They chased each other. They were happy.

Pat and the boy talked. The boy was named Sam. Sam was six. Pat was seven. They were almost the same age. They were not in the same class. But they could be friends.

The dogs came back. They were tired too. They drank water from a bowl. The bowl was on the grass. Pat had brought it.

The sun was high. The sun was warm. The sky was blue. The clouds were white. The grass was green. The trees were tall.

A man rode a bike past them. The bike had two wheels. The man went fast. Rex looked. Rex did not chase. Rex was a good dog. The white dog also did not chase. They were both good dogs.

Pat opened a bag. Pat had snacks. Pat had crackers. Sam had a juice box. They sat together. They ate. Rex looked at the crackers. Pat gave Rex one cracker. Rex ate it fast. The white dog wanted one too. Sam gave the white dog one. The white dog also ate it fast. Dogs eat fast.

A bird flew down. The bird saw the crackers. The bird wanted some. Pat broke a cracker. Pat threw a piece. The bird hopped over. The bird took the piece. The bird flew up to a tree. The bird ate the piece in the tree.

"That bird is smart," Sam said. "Yes," said Pat.

After a while, Sam said he had to go home. "It was nice to meet you," Sam said. "Yes," said Pat. "Nice to meet you too." They waved. Sam and the white dog walked away.

Pat and Rex stayed for a little longer. Then Pat said, "Time to go, Rex." Rex got up. Rex wagged his tail. They walked home together.

At home, Mom was in the kitchen. Mom was making soup. The soup smelled good. The soup was hot. Mom smiled when she saw Pat. "How was the park?" Mom asked. "Fun," said Pat. "I made a friend. His name is Sam."

"That is good," Mom said. "Wash your hands. Lunch is almost ready."

Pat washed his hands. The soap had bubbles. The water was warm. Pat dried his hands on a towel. The towel was soft.

Pat sat at the table. Mom put a bowl in front of him. The soup had carrots. The soup had peas. The soup had small pieces of chicken. Pat ate slowly. The soup was very hot.

Rex ate his food too. Rex had dog food in a bowl. The bowl was on the floor. Rex was a tidy dog. He did not spill.

After lunch, Pat colored a picture. The picture was of a dog. The picture was of Rex. Pat used brown for the body. Pat used black for the nose. Pat used pink for the tongue. The picture looked nice.

Mom hung the picture on the fridge. There were already three other pictures there. The new one made four.

Pat sat on the couch. Pat opened a book. The book had a map. The map had hills. The map had a forest. The map had a small house. The book was about a rabbit. The rabbit lived in the small house. Pat read about the rabbit.

The rabbit had a friend. The friend was a turtle. The rabbit was fast. The turtle was slow. They had a race. The turtle won. The turtle won because the rabbit took a nap. Pat thought that was funny.

After the book, Pat watched a show. The show was about animals. There was a lion. There was an elephant. There was a giraffe. The giraffe was the tallest.

Soon it was time for bed. Pat brushed his teeth. Pat put on pajamas. The pajamas had stars on them. Mom read him a book. The book was about the moon.

The moon was bright. The moon was round. The moon went across the sky every night.

Pat liked the book. Pat asked Mom to read it again. Mom read it again. Then Mom turned off the light.

"Good night, Pat," Mom said. "Good night, Mom," said Pat. "Good night, Rex," said Pat. Rex was on the rug next to the bed. Rex was already asleep.

Pat closed his eyes. The room was dark. The house was quiet. Outside, the wind moved the trees. The trees made a soft sound.

Soon Pat was asleep too. He dreamed about the park. He dreamed about Rex. He dreamed about Sam and the white dog. The dream was happy.

In the morning, Pat woke up. The sun was in the window. Rex was already up. Rex wagged his tail. Pat got out of bed and put on his shoes. They went outside again. It was a new day.
